Cooking basics

some simple facts to get you started

Knives

Saftey first!

always use a sharp knife when prepping. hold the handle firmly in your dominant hand and use your other hand to stableize what ever you are prepping. curl your other hand a claw and use the knuckle of your middle finger to guide the blade. this method helps to avoid injury.

Types of cuts

there are many types of cuts. learning the ones above will help you understand recipes and sound extra fancy to your friends!

Sharpening

get a good sharpener. Sharp knives are safer and much more effective than dull ones. using a sharp knife can also reduce burning eyes when cutting onions.

the wonders of the chefs knife

the chefs knife is the most important knife in the kitchen. so important in fact that it often called a kitchen knife. spending a little more on a good chefs knife will save you a lot of headache in the long run.

Heat

saute

sauteing is a great way to cook prepped veggies and small pieces of meat. use medium to high heat and some form of oil and frequently move the ingredients around until they have reached the desired level of doneness.

Oven roasting

Roasting is a good way to cook larger peices of meat or root vegitables. such as whole chickens. the trick to roasting is to follow the recipe to a tee. use a meat thermomoeter to make sure the meat is fully cooked through. there is nothing worse than cutting into a freshly roasted bird to find it is still raw inside.

shallow frying

if you want crispy chicken fingers or fries but you dont own an airfryer and dont want to use copious amounts of oil, shallow frying is a great option. an inch of oil in a frying pan and an oil thermomiter is all you need. just be careful. HOT OIL CAN BE EXTREMLY DANGEROUS so fry with care and be sure that the pan handle is positioned to the side and not tward you so you dont accidently bump it.

Ingredients

meat

there are many kinds of meats. meat is a great source of protine and is deceptivly easy to prepair. steak is simple. salt and pepper both sides and add butter to a screaming hot pan. sear the steak on both sides until a brown crust develops. make sure it is cooked through! undercooked meat can carry pathogines.

herbs

herbs add flavor and complexity to dishes. using herbs can make 2 dishes with the same meat and veggies tast completly diffrent. expiriment with combinations of diffrent herbs and see what kind of delicious creations you can concoct.

Fruits and veggies

veggies are packed with vitimins and minierals and are absolutly necissary to a healthy diet. But healthy doesnt need to mean bland. roasting is a great method for prepairing a veggie side for your steak. medium diced carrots, potatoes onions and garlic on a baking sheet with a drizzle of oil, salt and pepper baked at 450 until cooked through will fill you up and keep in the fridge for days.

sauces

sauces are a great way to define a dish. think dressings, soy sauce, fish sauce, hot sauce ect. these ingredients can help define the flavor profile of a dish and give it a kick.

Flavors

sweet

sugars are a common sourse of sweet flavor. natural sugars occur in sweet fruits. sweetness can help balance sour or bitter flavors to make them more palletable.

Sour

Acids are the main source of sour flavors. lemons and vinigar, are both very acidic. the addition of sourness can add excitment to a dish when applied spairingly. lemon juice or vinigar is commonly found on seafood and chicken.

Savory/salty

salt is the secret weapon of the chef. it may seem obvious, but too many times I have been speaking to an inexpirenced cook and I hear that they had veggies meat and herbs in a dish but it still tasted bland. My first question is always "did you add salt?" and often times they didnt. salt is a flavor enhancer. use it in every dish including sweet dishes.

bitter

bitterness is usually considered a negative thing when talking about food but some of the most delicious food uses bitter ingredients to create complex flavors. Cocao is a great example of a very bitter ingreidnet that, when combined and ballanced with the other flavors on this list can become Chocolate, or mole sauce.
